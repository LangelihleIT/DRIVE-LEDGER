ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This is a personal record of my volunteer work with DKMS, the world’s largest stem cell donor organisation across the KwaZulu-Natal corridor during my 2026 gap year.
Every node marks a real moment, a conversation, a sign-up, an opportunity to change a life. Each node represents a real visit: a campus activation, a boardroom session, a pre-race briefing.
The locations change but mission stays the same: increase the donor pool, increase the chances of a match. By making each step visible, this system turns individual effort into collective, trackable impact.
